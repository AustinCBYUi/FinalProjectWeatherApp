Information

Some data may be inaccurate to specific search types. For example, while searching coordinates, the times may be inaccurate or just completely broken. This is simply due to the API not supporting that data in the response.

OpenWeather API is quite a reliable source, but as always please take all information from this application with a firm understanding that things do change, and environmental factors are unpredictable.

Thank you for using the Weather Application!
